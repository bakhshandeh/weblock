The Saltshakers

The Saltshakers were one of the first bands I met when I moved to Milwaukee about 7 years ago. They recently celebrated their 10 year anniversary with a new website launch. I've had the honor of doing their last 3 websites and have always been given creative freedom. It's so nice to work with people who have an open mind and an artistic eye.
